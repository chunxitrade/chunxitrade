FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and mobile applications.

How can I download your mobile apps?

Our mobile applications are available for download on both Google Play Store for Android devices and Apple App Store for iOS devices. Simply search for our app name in the respective app store, or visit our website for direct download links.

What types of products do you offer?

We offer a wide range of products including daily necessities, fashion and apparel, electronics, building materials, agricultural products, and licensed food products. Our e-commerce platform provides convenient online shopping with international shipping options.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we provide international trade services and shipping to many countries worldwide. Our logistics partners ensure safe and timely delivery. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Contact us for specific shipping inquiries.

How can I become a business partner?

We welcome partnership inquiries from businesses worldwide. Please reach out to our enterprise client team at weimiaomiao@chunxitrade.com to discuss potential collaboration opportunities.

What marketing services do you provide?

We offer comprehensive marketing services including exhibition planning, marketing strategy development, brand promotion, and digital marketing solutions. Our team helps businesses reach their target audiences effectively across multiple channels.

How can I get technical support for apps?

For technical support regarding our mobile applications, please email support@chunxitrade.com with details about your issue. Our support team typically responds within 24 hours and is available to assist with troubleshooting.
